About FED — For Every Driver Verified

Mission: FED is a safety-focused platform that empowers passengers to confirm driver identity before and during rides.

Reporting policy: FED takes reports seriously. False reporting or malicious submissions are strictly prohibited.

This demo is local-only — in production reports would be stored securely on a server.
